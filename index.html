Get to Know Me

Hello, I'm Corrinne. I am a graduate from the University of Central Florida who studied Digital Media, focusing on web design. Currently, I am a web developer in the Central Florida area.

My strengths and passions lie in frontend web technologies, but I also love traditional and digital art and have a passion for education. I hope to apply my knowledge with technology and the web in an educational setting in the future.

Some of my hobbies include playing video games, tasting various craft beers, and doing nail art, which can be seen at my personal blog, Galactic Lacquer.
