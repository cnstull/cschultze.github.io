New Online Limited Submission

The Office of Research & Commercialization (ORC) is pleased to announce the launch of a web-based system for the submission and review of all upcoming limited submission (institutional limit) grant programs. Research faculty can now submit their white papers for all limited submission programs using their ARGIS log-in. ORC will routinely send out notifications of limited submission programs with internal deadlines for white paper submittals to all faculty and staff with ARGIS accounts. If you do not currently have an ARGIS account, please contact webmasteroor@mail.ucf.edu to request one.

Please visit http://argis.research.ucf.edu/LimitedSubmissions/ to sign in and view the list of all open limited submission programs.

New Proposal Information

NSF and NIH have instituted important proposal changes effective this month. You can read about the latest NSF clarifications here: NSF GPG Summary

In addition, effective January 25, NIH, AHRQ and NIOSH have eliminated the error correction window for applications. For further information on this important change, see: http://grants.nih.gov/grants/guide/notice-files/NOT-OD-10-123.html

And, effective January 1, NSF is requiring a supplement describing data management. This is similar to the postdoc mentoring requirement, except it is a supplement and not required in the proposal. This supplement should describe how the proposal will conform to NSF policy on the dissemination and sharing of research results.
