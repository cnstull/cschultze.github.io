Millionaires

The UCF millionaires list was started in 2000 to celebrate the accomplishments of UCF's researchers, mirroring the kind of support universities provide their sports teams. This year marks the 10th edition, which recognized researchers in areas ranging from education to simulation to photonics.

The sole requirement for entry into the exclusive order is externally sponsored research funding of $1 million or more during a given fiscal year.
