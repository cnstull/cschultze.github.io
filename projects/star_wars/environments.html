The following environments were used for design inspiration:

Tatooine

The desert planet and Luke's home

Cantina

The bar where Luke meets Han

Endor

A planet with tall forests that is also home of the Ewoks

Death Star

The space station built by Darth Vader, Palpatine, and the Empire

Asteroid with Space Slug

The asteroid where the Space Slug resides, which "eats" the Milennium Falcon

Sarlacc Pit

The pit where the Sarlacc lives on the planet Tatooine

Hoth

The icy planet where the Rebel base is located

Petranaki Arena

A fighting and execution arena on the planet Geonosis

Cloud City

Built far above the surface and clouds of the planet Bespin
