Star Wars Adventures is a new interactive venue for all ages, featuring many elements and concepts from the Star Wars universe.

Components will be based off of the detailed planets and spaces featured in the Star Wars films. Connections will be made to make visitors feel as if they are a key player in the Star Wars world themselves, immersing them in the classic storyline, as well as many interactive games, rides, and shows.

My idea for Star Wars Adventures is inspired heavily by the original trilogy (A New Hope, The Empire Strikes Back, and Return of the Jedi), but will still incorporate some elements from the prequels as well (The Phantom Menace, Attack of the Clones, and Revenge of the Sith).

Star Wars is property of George Lucas.
